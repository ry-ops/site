## About

I also administer both networks and systems for one of the top <a href="https://www.startribune.com/minnesota-top-nonprofits/600006951/100</a> non-profits in Minnesota. I'm based in Superior, WI. 

This little site (which started as a blog nearly 15 years ago) is an intersection of a whole host of interests for me. It's my design outlet, code foundry, and a place to create the things I want to see in the world.
